# Elekeeper / SAJ eSolar Inverters

Want to know exactly how much energy your solar panels are generating? The Elekeeper app connects your SAJ eSolar inverter directly to your Homey. Monitor your current power output and keep track of your daily and total energy yield in real-time, right from your Homey dashboard.

With the built-in Flow cards, you can easily automate your home based on your solar production. Turn on the washing machine when the sun is shining brightly, or get a notification when you've hit your daily energy goal. Simply log in with your existing Elekeeper account, and Homey will handle the rest!

## Setup Instructions
1. Add a new device in Homey and select the Elekeeper Inverter.
2. Log in using your standard Elekeeper/SAJ username and password.
3. Your inverter will be discovered automatically and your live data will start flowing.
